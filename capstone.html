MedDash - A Patient Facing Dashboard

Senior Capstone Project the University of California, San Diego

Written on 05/02/2025

It is no secret that the older we get, the more likely it is that we will develop a chronic condition. Despite being able to track our health data with smartphones, watches, and rings, many elderly patients have trouble integrating this information into meaningful insights. This is why I, along with a team of five other students, built MedDash, a patient-facing dashboard that allows elderly patients to view their health data in a more user-friendly way. Please read more on the MedDash Website, watch our project video, or check out our poster that we presented at UCSD's 2024 Bioengineering Day!
