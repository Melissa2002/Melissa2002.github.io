Cloud Engineering Intern

Codex Health

Written on 07/26/2025

I interned with Codex Health during the summers of 2023 and 2024. This Palo Alto based startup is working to improve connectivity between patient and provider for better diabetic management. During my time there, I worked on the backend of the platform, using Golang and Google Cloud Platform to build RESTful APIs.

I also had the opportunity to learn software engineering common practices, including pull requests, agile methodologies, and provisioning infrastructure using Terraform. My favorite part of the internship was learning how to shift between different tasks and seeing my work deployed to production!
